ℹ️ Solver Penalty & Weights Information

The solver searches for assignments that minimize total penalty points. Penalties are calculated based on examiner competence levels for each student's required thesis categories (multiplied by the category weight):

  • 'Can' Competence: 0 penalty points (optimal match).
  • 'Could' Competence: Incurs the 'Could' weight penalty (Default: 3).
  • 'Cannot' Competence: Incurs the 'Cannot' weight penalty (Default: 10).
  • Group Mismatch: Incurs a penalty (Default: 5) if coupled projects do not share the same examiner.
  • Unoccupied Examiner: Incurs a penalty per student short of the target minimum capacity ratio (4:2, 3:2, 2:1, 1:1) (Default: 4).

Note: If a student's thesis category has a specific weight set in constraints, it scales these penalties. Otherwise, the category's default weight (from the Constraints tab) is used.
